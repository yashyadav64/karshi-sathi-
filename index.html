About Us

We are Kisan Saathi – one of the prominent players in FPO model engagement established in 2015. An initiative to build FPO's of India – creating self sustainability and better farm income realisation for farmers, FPO's through effective capacity building, FPO Management, collectivisation approach, agronomy, cultivating practices, transfer of technology, improved post harvest management and efficient front end market linkages.
